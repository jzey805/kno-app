## 🌟 What is Kno?
Kno isn't just a place to store links; it’s a workspace for deep thinking. It helps you digest complex information through AI-driven quizzes, visual organization, and advanced logic analysis. Whether you are a researcher, a student, or a creator, Kno turns a messy "feed" into a powerful personal "brain."

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 📥 The Entry Point: Inbox & Library
* **Smart Inbox:** Upload files or paste links. Kno automatically generates summaries and multi-choice quizzes to test your understanding instantly.
* **Structured Library:** Organize your world into **Sources**, **Folders**, and **Themes**. Use colors and custom naming to make your knowledge workspace yours.

### 🧠 Deep Integration: Brain & Memory
* **The Brain:** The central command center. Every source tracks its own quiz history, AI summaries, and your personal thoughts. Visualize how sources link to folders and themes.
* **Memory Palace:** Linked directly to the Brain. Track your score for every source, see which quizzes you should redo, and view a chronological timeline of when your knowledge was created.

### ⚡ AI Cognitive Tools (The Whiteboard Housekeepers)
* **Neural Dump:** Your creative assistant. It can generate images or create entire learning paths based on the sources you select.
* **Logic Guide:** The "Truth Scanner." It analyzes sources for **Factual Accuracy**, **Cognitive Balance**, and **Logical Integrity**.
* **Ko (AI Secretary):** Ask questions about one or many sources. Ko finds the answers and saves them directly to the relevant source.

### 🔬 Idea Synthesis
* **Alchemy:** Combine two or more sources to create a brand-new perspective or idea.
* **Collider:** Conflict resolution. Put sources with differing opinions together to analyze the friction between ideas.
* **Spark:** The matchmaker. Kno suggests connections between sources that you might have missed.

### 🛠️ Workspace Mastery
* **Arrange:** One-click tidy up. Automatically organize your whiteboard or group sources into themes.
* **Export:** Take your work anywhere. Export your entire whiteboard to **Markdown** or **PDF**.
